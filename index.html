About

A technology leader who builds teams, delivers BMS & RMS platforms at scale, and turns complex broadcast challenges into measurable outcomes.

On Leadership: I lead cross-functional teams of engineers, implementation specialists, and client success managers — setting technical direction, defining KPIs, and building a culture where people take ownership. I report directly to the Board, own the technology budget and roadmap, and conduct C-level business reviews with clients across four continents. Over 14 years, I grew from an individual contributor to Head of Media Technology — a trajectory built on trust, results, and the ability to develop the people around me.

On BMS Implementation: I have led 10+ end-to-end Broadcast Management System deployments for organisations including Sony Pictures Networks, Viacom18, SABC, Warner Bros, and Tata Play. My BMS expertise covers broadcast scheduling, ad sales traffic management, rights clearance, EPG generation, and playout integration. From greenfield rollouts to complex migrations (including BroadView transitions and AWS cloud migrations), I ensure every implementation delivers operational reliability and commercial value.

On RMS & ERP Integration: I architect and deliver Rights Management System implementations that give broadcasters full control over content licensing, territorial rights, and compliance. I also bridge RMS and BMS with enterprise ERP systems — integrating SAP for finance, invoicing, amortisation, and revenue assurance. This end-to-end approach eliminates silos and gives leadership a single source of truth across rights, scheduling, and revenue.

On Impact: My teams have reduced operational costs by 40% through automation and process re-engineering, achieved 99.9% broadcast uptime through HA/DR architecture, and increased response efficiency by 30% through AI-powered workflows and self-service portals. I've delivered solutions across India, UAE, South Africa, and Papua New Guinea — always with a disciplined, client-first approach.
